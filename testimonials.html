Testimonials A few words from our campers, parents, and volunteers...

Mom of Camper, 2013

"I am just in awe of how well everything went. It was obvious that a lot of preparation and coordination went in to making this day as great as it was. Thank YOU!!"

Camp Counselor, 2013

"The best part of the day I think for me with the kids was doing the rock wall and seeing the faces on the kids when they accomplished it."

Camp Counselor, 2013

"This was one of the best things I have ever done. Not only am I a person that absolutely adores children.. but I love the Camp and EVERYTHING that it stands for."

Mom of Camper, 2013

"The medals at the end of the day were awesome. She comes from a pretty athletic family. Due to her having 19-20 seizures a minute we have had to pull her from baseball and soccer. The sun was too much for her. However, she is becoming a great volleyball player. She was so proud to be voted most athletic. She couldn't wait to get in the car and call her dad."

Camp Counselor, 2013

"I can't wait to help next year, thank you for giving me this chance to open my eyes up to something that I have found a new passion for."

Mom of Camper, 2013

"It was an emotional day seeing all the different kids, their skill levels and abilities. Epilepsy takes on so many forms. We appreciate the time you took to make it special and meaningful and pack it full of activities. My daughter loved the zip line and art stuff. And getting the medal was priceless. How many times has she been passed up by her sister and brother.. who get all the hardware and glory. Thanks for pairing them up with other similar kids and making them all feel special. Can't wait for next year."

Mom of Camper, 2013

"My daughter was a reluctant participant. She said she didn’t want to be branded an “epileptic kid”. She was also nervous that she wouldn’t be able to fully participate and therefore be bored out of her gourd! However – once she got to the campsite, she was excited and willing to try it all. She was VERY thrilled by her success on the Zipline and the Rock Wall. She definitely sees herself in a new way now and I see a much more confident young woman."
