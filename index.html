EXPLORE what worry free fun is all about

Meg Busing, a Registered Nurse, created Camp YouCan in 2011 following a 10 year battle with intractable epilepsy and brain surgery. Meg was in a car accident in highschool and acquired a life altering Traumatic Brain Injury. Meg made a successful recover after physical, occupational, and speech therapy and graduated from highschool. Five years later...

Read More..

Take a tour through 2013 Camp YouCan.. We can show you how our campers have fun, build self-confidence, try new things, and make memories for a lifetime. By providing a safe enviroment for our campers through our experienced volunteers and medical professionals, we are able to give parents and children a worry free experience.
